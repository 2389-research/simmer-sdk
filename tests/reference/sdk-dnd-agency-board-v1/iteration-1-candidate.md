# The Tides of Saltmarrow

**Setting:** Saltmarrow (~400 people, Cerulean Coast). Empty nets. The docks reek of copper and rot. Children aren't allowed near the water.

---

## The Situation

Seven days of bones instead of fish — human, animal, picked white. Three days ago **Mayor Aldric Voss** (58, heavyset, desperate) vanished. Yesterday, fisherman **Torben Ash** stumbled into the **Broken Anchor** muttering about *"singing in the deep."*

**Harbormaster Mira Canto** (pragmatic, sleep-deprived, personally losing money) hires the party for 400gp: find Voss, stop the dying. **High tide is in six hours.** After that, she says, she's not sure the town will still be here to pay.

---

## What's Really Happening

Mayor Voss, facing bankruptcy, traded "the bounty of the bay" to awaken **Seraveth** — an undead cultist sealed in **Deepholm**, a drowned Umberlee temple beneath the bay — not realizing he was offering every life in the water. Voss is now chained to the altar while Seraveth completes a ritual to raise a drowned army, finishing at high tide. His assistant **Nella Bright** supplied the ritual tablet and knows everything.

---

## Three Paths In

**Path A — Follow the Mayor:** Nella (nervous, guilty) can be persuaded or pressured to reveal Voss rowed to desk-map coordinates three nights ago. She resists until the party makes her understand people will die.

**Path B — Follow the Bones:** Torben Ash, once sobered (DC 12 Persuasion or a flask), describes a cold, copper-smelling calm patch a mile out. He'll row them there; his diving barrel gets them down. DC 14 Nature/Survival confirms a submerged structure.

**Path C — The Tide-Reader:** **Oswen** (blind, 70, east dock) reads this morning's bone formation as a summoning diagram. She knows Deepholm's history and Seraveth's weakness: a seal glyph above the temple door. Invoking it (costs one spell slot) prevents Seraveth's death burst. Her price: retrieve her drowned son's ring from the ruins.

---

## Deepholm (Rising Water Throughout)

**Entry Hall** — knee-deep and rising; 5 skeletons (MM p. 272), DC 13 Athletics to move freely.

**The Choir Chamber** — four drowned cultists chanting around a cracked resonance stone. Choose before entering: **(1) Silence stealthily** (DC 14 Stealth, neutralize one at a time — flood holds) **or (2) Shatter the stone** (one action, stops chanting instantly, but the crack echoes through the temple — Seraveth gets a readied action before initiative is rolled).

**Seraveth's Sanctum** — Voss chained to the altar, barely conscious. Seraveth (undead priest; use Mummy Lord, MM p. 227) fights in deep water with darkness and a grapple-and-drown attack. Without Oswen's glyph: DC 15 Con, 4d10 necrotic on death.

---

## Consequences Matter

- **Save Voss + seal:** Town survives. Before leaving the sanctum, the party decides: **expose Voss's deal publicly, or keep it secret?** Nella begs silence.
- **Seal without saving Voss:** Temple locked, Voss dies inside. Mira pays; Nella never forgives them.
- **Save Voss, don't seal:** Army reduced to stragglers — Seraveth reforms in thirty days.
- **Fail the timer:** Twelve drowned warriors rise. Final battle on the docks at dawn, town burning behind them.
