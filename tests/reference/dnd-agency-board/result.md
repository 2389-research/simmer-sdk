# The Bones of Saltmire

## One-Shot Adventure for 4 Level-5 Characters (3-4 Hours)

### Setup

Saltmire clings to a chalk cliff on the Straaden Coast. For seven days, every net has surfaced tangled with humanoid bones. Yesterday, Mayor **Elda Thresh** vanished -- door locked from inside, salt water on the floor, claw marks gouging the windowsill. Her daughter **Maren Thresh** (lobster-boat captain, pragmatic, terrified) hires the party: 200 gp and Elda's *cloak of the manta ray* if they bring her back alive. Elda dies at high tide -- six hours from arrival.

### The Interruption

Roughly 90 minutes in, regardless of thread, a **drowned one** (AC 14, HP 45, slam +4/1d8+2 + DC 13 Con or paralyzed 1 min) erupts from the harbor and drags **Tomas**, a 12-year-old net-mender, underwater. They must choose: dive in now (DC 14 Athletics, disadvantage on non-aquatic attacks underwater) or stay on task and hear Tomas's mother screaming his name for the rest of the session. Saving Tomas costs 30+ minutes of the clock. Failing to act makes Maren and all townsfolk refuse further help.

### Three Threads, Three Climaxes

**Thread A -- The Sea Caves.** Fisherman **Corvin Blackweed** (gruff, three-fingered, drinks at the Keel & Cork) saw something dragging a body into the water. He talks for 10 gp or DC 13 Persuasion. He points to **flooded sea caves** accessible only at low tide (4 hours from arrival). Inside: a coral-cage shrine to **The Unraveler**, tended by 3 drowned ones.

*Mid-thread fork:* One drowned one, **Neshka**, still remembers her living name. She offers to unlock the cage holding Elda and turn on her masters -- if the party destroys the shrine's anchor-stone, severing the Unraveler's hold on this coast permanently. Destroying the stone (AC 17, HP 30, immune to nonmagical weapons) takes 2+ rounds while the other drowned ones attack. Refusing Neshka means fighting all 3 (CR 6 total). Accepting means fighting only 2 but permanently closing the shrine -- Maren loses a potential bargaining chip with the sea.

**Climax A:** Storm the shrine before the tide returns (6 rounds once water enters). Neshka ally path: 2 drowned ones (AC 14, HP 45 each). Brute force path: 3 drowned ones + anchor-stone intact.

**Thread B -- The Tidespeaker Chapel.** Investigation at Elda's house (DC 14) uncovers a ledger: she paid someone called "Brother Silt" monthly for two years. The trail leads to a shuttered cliffside church. **Brother Silt** (merfolk warlock; AC 15, HP 58, eldritch blast +7/1d10+4, *hold person* 1/day) has been converting townsfolk into amphibious servants. He offers water-breathing in exchange for loyalty.

*Mid-thread fork:* Silt brings the party to Elda -- who says she doesn't want to leave. Her lungs are failing; Silt's ritual is the only thing keeping her alive. She begs the party to tell Maren she's dead. The party must choose: honor Elda's wish (break the contract, Maren gets no closure, Elda lives), drag Elda out against her will (she has days to live on land without Silt's magic), or negotiate with Silt for a portable version of the ritual (DC 17 Persuasion -- he demands the party eliminate Vorrow as payment).

**Climax B:** If negotiation fails or the party attacks: Silt + 2 converted townsfolk (AC 12, HP 22 each). Turning converts against Silt requires DC 16 Persuasion, halving his forces.

**Thread C -- The Bone Tomb.** DC 13 Medicine/Arcana on the dock bones reveals they are centuries old, recently disturbed. Maren's boat reaches a **collapsed underwater tomb** one mile out where **Vorrow**, a sea hag (AC 14, HP 52, claw +5/2d8+3, *Horrifying Visage* DC 13 Wis or frightened), dredges corpses to build a bone golem (AC 9, HP 93, slam +7/2d8+4, immune nonmagical bludg/pierc/slash). **Climax C:** Vorrow offers Elda's location for Brother Silt's literal heart. Accept and she teleports the party to Silt for assassination -- or refuse, fight Vorrow plus the half-finished golem (CR 7), and find a scrying pool revealing Elda's exact location.

### Faction Fallout

Whichever thread resolves the rescue, the surviving faction retaliates within the hour. Destroyed the shrine? Vorrow sends 2 bone constructs (AC 9, HP 40 each) into town. Killed Silt? His converts sabotage the docks. Killed Vorrow? The bone tide intensifies -- 1d4 drowned ones surface at the harbor. The party must handle one final 2-round skirmish (CR 4) or bargain with the surviving power at a terrible price.

### Key NPCs

| NPC | Motivation | Betrays party if... |
|---|---|---|
| Maren Thresh | Save her mother | Party ignores Tomas or abandons search |
| Brother Silt | Convert Saltmire to serve The Unraveler | Party sides with Vorrow |
| Vorrow (sea hag) | Build golem army, destroy Silt | Party sides with Silt or destroys bones |
| Corvin Blackweed | Survive, drink, be left alone | Threatened or cheated |
| Neshka (drowned one) | Remember who she was | Party refuses to destroy the anchor-stone |
