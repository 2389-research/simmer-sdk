# The Bones of Saltmire

## One-Shot Adventure for 4 Level-5 Characters (3-4 Hours)

### Setup

The fishing village of Saltmire clings to a crumbling chalk cliff on the Straaden Coast. For seven days, every net hauled from the bay has come up tangled with humanoid bones -- femurs, ribcages, skulls with barnacles fused to the teeth. The catch has stopped entirely. Yesterday, Mayor Elda Thresh vanished from her home. Her door was locked from the inside. Salt water pooled on her bedroom floor, and claw marks gouged the windowsill from the outside.

The party arrives at dawn, hired by Elda's daughter, **Maren Thresh** (early 20s, lobster-boat captain, pragmatic, terrified). She offers 200 gp and her mother's *cloak of the manta ray* if they bring Elda back alive.

### Three Threads (Party Chooses Where to Pull)

**Thread A -- The Docks.** Old fisherman **Corvin Blackweed** (gruff, missing three fingers, drinks at the Keel & Cork tavern) saw something dragging a body into the water two nights ago. He'll talk for 10 gp or a DC 13 Persuasion check. He points the party to the **sea caves beneath the cliff** -- accessible only at low tide (4 hours from arrival, ticking clock). Inside: a flooded shrine to a forgotten deep-sea entity called **The Unraveler**, tended by **drowned ones** (use wight stat block, 3 of them). Elda is here, alive, caged in coral, being prepared as a sacrifice. Combat encounter: CR 5-6.

**Thread B -- The Mayor's House.** Investigation (DC 14 Investigation) reveals a hidden ledger in Elda's desk. She has been paying monthly sums to someone called "Brother Silt" for two years. The salt water and claw marks suggest she was taken by force -- but the payments suggest she knew her captors. Following the money trail leads to the **Tidespeaker Chapel**, a shuttered church on the cliff edge. **Brother Silt** (merfolk warlock of the Great Old One, cunning, believes he is saving the town) has been converting townsfolk into amphibious servants. He offers the party a deal: join his cause and gain water-breathing permanently, or fight. If they negotiate (DC 16 Persuasion), he reveals Elda came willingly at first but tried to back out. He will release her -- if the party destroys the rival shrine in the sea caves (Thread A location). This creates a moral choice: help one evil to stop another?

**Thread C -- The Bones Themselves.** A DC 13 Medicine or Arcana check on the bones at the docks reveals they are centuries old but surfaced recently -- something disturbed them. A DC 15 Nature check identifies deep-trench currents pushing upward. If the party follows this thread (requiring a boat from Maren), they discover a **collapsed underwater tomb** one mile offshore where a **sea hag named Vorrow** is dredging corpses to build a bone golem (use flesh golem stats, reskinned). Vorrow knows where Elda is and will trade the information for Brother Silt's heart -- literally. She and Silt are rival servants of The Unraveler, each jockeying for dominance.

### Convergence

All threads lead to a climactic choice at the **flooded shrine** during low tide (the 4-hour clock applies regardless of path). The party must decide:

- **Destroy the shrine**, ending the bone tide but enraging Vorrow, who attacks (sea hag + 2 water weirds, CR 6).
- **Ally with Brother Silt**, gaining his help against Vorrow but leaving his conversion cult intact.
- **Ally with Vorrow**, gaining her bone golem as muscle against Silt but enabling a monster.
- **Fight everyone**, hardest path (all enemies, CR 7-8), but frees Saltmire completely.

Elda survives unless the party takes more than 6 hours total (the sacrifice completes at high tide). Maren turns hostile if her mother dies.

### Key NPCs at a Glance

| NPC | Motivation | Will betray party if... |
|---|---|---|
| Maren Thresh | Save her mother at any cost | Party abandons the search |
| Corvin Blackweed | Survive, drink, be left alone | Threatened or cheated |
| Brother Silt | Convert Saltmire, serve The Unraveler | Party sides with Vorrow |
| Vorrow (sea hag) | Build her golem army, destroy Silt | Party sides with Silt or destroys bones |
